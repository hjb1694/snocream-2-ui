What is Snocream?

Sno Cream is an inspired name from Subling, a popular south east Asian shaved milk dessert which is originally from Korea. The original name for the similar dessert is Bingsu. Using condensed milk and yummy flavors, a sweet snow is produced. Topped with fresh fruits and other delicious goodies, a truly unique dessert is crafted. Come experience one of Knoxville's most loved treats!
